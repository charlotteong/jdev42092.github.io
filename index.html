Surving Snow in Boston's (Data) Shadows

In the pass several years, extreme weather events, particularly winter storms, have hit Boston. In response, communication of weather advisories, public service suspensions, and municipal responses have moved from more traditional networks to social ones- particularly Twitter. While Twitter has branded itself as a platform of social democracy, prior research has shown that there are significant gaps in representation of both users and tweets relating to historically underserved communities. These gaps, known as data shadows, are critical to identify in order to equitably serving all communities in the city.
